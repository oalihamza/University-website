Master level Programs

M.S/M.Phil course will be for four semesters. In first two semesters, 24 credit hours course work and in 3rd and 4th semesters 6 credit hours research work / internship / project (as the case may be) will be carried out. For MS/ M.Phil leading to Ph.D. student shall be required to acquire CGPA = 3.0 and to pass a comprehensive examination .

PHD level Programs
